
A mixture of Co(OAc)2·4H2O (25 mg, 0.1 mmol), L1 (32 mg, 0.1 mmol), DEF (4 mL) and deionized water (4 mL) was sealed in a 20 mL borosilicate screw vial and then heated to 80 °C for 24 h; red crystals were obtained in 60% yield. IR (KBr, cm−1): 3364(m), 1683(m), 1577(s), 1508(m), 1375(s), 1236(m), 1134(w), 862(w), 764(m).
