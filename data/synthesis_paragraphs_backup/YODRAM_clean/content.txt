‡ Synthesis of [Co9(int)18(H2O)4(H2O)2]·5.5DMF: The mixture of Co(NO3)2·6H2O (0.86 mmol, 0.250 g), isonicotinic acid (IN, 0.85 mmol, 0.104 g) and 4 ml N,N-dimethyl formamide (DMF) and 2 ml EtOH was sealed in a 20 ml vial and heated to 100 °C for 1 day, and then cooled to room-temperature.
