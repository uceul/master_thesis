A mixture of Cd(NO3)2·4H2O (15 mg, 0.05 mmol), Htpim (15 mg, 0.05 mmol), d-H2ca (10 mg, 0.05 mmol), MeOH (9 mL), DMF (1 mL), and H2O (0.2 mL) was placed in a Teflon-lined stainless steel container, heated at 140 °C for 75 h, and cooled to room temperature. The crystals were collected, washed by distilled water, and dried in a vacuum. Yield: 74%. Anal. Calc. for C76H90N10O23Cd4: C, 46.54; H, 4.63; N, 7.14. Found: C, 46.80; H, 4.47; N, 6.99%.
