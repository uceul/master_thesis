A 20 mL vial containing CuI (0.095 g, 0.5 mmol), isonicotinic acid (0.062, 0.5 mmol), Mn(CH3COO)2·4H2O (0.080 g, 2 mmol), 3 mL DMF, and 1.5 mL ethanol was sealed and heated in an oven at 100 °C for 3 days. The vial was taken out and naturally cooled to room temperature. After being washed with fresh DMF, yellow club-shaped crystals were obtained in pure phase (Yield: 35 mg, 15% based on CuI).
