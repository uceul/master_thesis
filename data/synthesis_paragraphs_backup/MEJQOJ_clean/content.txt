
The mixture of Zn(NO3)2 (0.5 mmol, 0.150 g), adenine (0.5 mmol, 0.070 g), isonicotinic acid (0.5 mmol, 0.06 g), 1-methyl-2-pyrrolidinone (3 ml), 1,3-dimethylurea (2 g) and methanol (3 ml) was sealed in a 20 ml vial and heated to 120 °C for 3 days, and then cooled to room-temperature. The colorless crystals were obtained in pure phase, washed with water and ethanol, and dried at room temperature (yield: 75%). Anal. Calcd for C16H17N7O3Zn (420.74): C, 45.68; H, 4.07; N, 23.30. Found: C, 45.72; H, 3.95; N, 23.35%. IR (KBr cm−1): m = 3125 (s), 1610 (vs), 1580 (s), 1520 (m), 1375 (s), 1305 (s), 1070 (s), 960 (w), 780 (m), 660 (w).
