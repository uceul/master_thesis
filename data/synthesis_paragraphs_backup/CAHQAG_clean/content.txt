
A mixture of Mn(OAc)2·4H2O (73.5 mg, 0.3 mmol), H4L1 (20.3 mg, 0.05 mmol), bpfp (29.6 mg, 0.1 mmol) and H2O (10 mL) was sealed in a 23 mL Teflon-lined autoclave and heated at 150 °C for 3 days. After cooling to room temperature at a speed of 0.1 °C min−1, the colorless crystals of 1 (90.12 mg) were obtained and washed with distilled water. 61% yield based on Mn. Elemental analysis for C39H46Mn2N4O19, calcd (%): C, 47.53; H, 4.67; N, 5.68. Found (%): C, 47.48; H, 4.73; N, 5.62.
