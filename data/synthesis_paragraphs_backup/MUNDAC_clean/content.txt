A mixture of H2PDC (0.008 g, 0.047 mmol), CuI (0.016 g, 0.084 mmol), Dabco (0.010 g, 0.089 mmol), HNO3 (200 μL, 2.7 M in DMF), and DMF (1 mL) was sealed in a 20 mL vial and kept in an oven at 85 °C for 48 h, and then cooled to room temperature.
