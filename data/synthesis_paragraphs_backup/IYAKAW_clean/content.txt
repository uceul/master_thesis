
A mixture of Cd(OAc)2·2H2O (99 mg), 9-AC (55 mg), H2BDC (42 mg), Mg(OH)2 (7 mg), methanol (1 mL) and DMA (4 mL) were mixed and sealed in a 25 mL Teflon-lined stainless steel autoclave and heated at 150 °C for 4 days. The autoclave was subsequently cooled to room temperature. Colourless crystals and unknown powders were obtained.
