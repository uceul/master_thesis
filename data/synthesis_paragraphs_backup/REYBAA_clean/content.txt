
A mixture of ZnCl2 (0.020 g, 0.15 mmol), H4L1 (0.023 g, 0.05 mmol), pdp (0.018 g, 0.10 mmol), H2O (6 mL), and DMF (2 mL) was sealed in a Teflon-lined stainless steel vessel (15 mL), which was heated at 140 °C for 3 days and then cooled to room temperature at a rate of 10 °C h−1.
