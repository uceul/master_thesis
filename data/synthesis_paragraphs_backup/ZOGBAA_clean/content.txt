
In(NO3)·xH2O (20 mg) and H4DBIP (10 mg, 0.03 mmol) were dissolved in a 20 mL vial with DMF (1 mL) and HNO3 (0.5 mL, 2.7 M in DMF), the mixture was then stirred for 30 min and sealed. After being kept at 85 °C for 12 h, the reaction mixture was cooled to room temperature, the light brown block crystals were collected, washed with DMF, and air dried. Anal.
