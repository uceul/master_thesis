A mixture of CoSO4·7H2O (28.3 mg, 0.10 mmol), 5-phosphononicotinic acid (20.3 mg, 0.1 mmol), DABCO (16.8 mg, 0.15 mmol) and H2O (10 mL) was mixed and stirred for 20 min then transferred to a Teflon-lined stainless steel vessel (25 mL) and heated at 140 °C for 2 days. After slowly cooling to room temperature, small block purple crystals were obtained. Yield: 0.0087 g (36.2% based on Co). Anal. found (calcd) for C12H22N2O18P2Co3: C, 19.92 (19.99); H, 3.02(3.08); N, 3.88 (3.89)%.
