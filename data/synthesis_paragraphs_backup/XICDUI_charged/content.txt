‡ Preparation of compound 1. Solvothermal (1.5 ml benzene and 0.5 ml CHCl3) treatment of [Cu(I)(MeCN)4](BF4) (0.2 mmol) and LTZ (0.2 mmol) for three days at 100 °C afforded a colorless plate crystalline product (one pure phase). The yield of 1 was about 40% based on LTZ (found: C, 38.71; H, 2.21; N, 12.81. Calc.: C, 38.92; H, 2.16; N, 12.61%).
