
The mixture of Zn(NO3)2 (0.5 mmol, 0.150 g), adenine (0.5 mmol, 0.070 g), isonicotinic acid (0.5 mmol, 0.06 g), 2-imidazolidone hemihydrate (2 g) and H2O (1 ml) was sealed in a 20 ml vial and heated to 120 °C for 3 days, and then cooled to room-temperature. The colorless crystals were obtained in pure phase, washed with water and ethanol, and dried at room temperature (yield: 60%). Anal. Calcd for C14H14N8O3Zn (407.70): C, 41.24; H, 3.46; N, 27.48. Found: C, 41.72; H, 3.51; N, 27.40%. IR (KBr cm−1): m = 3125 (s), 1610 (s), 1580 (m), 1524 (s), 1375 (s), 1300 (m), 1071 (s), 960 (w), 780 (m), 504 (w).
