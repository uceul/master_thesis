
The mixture of CdCl2·6H2O (45 mg, 0.2 mmol), and NTB (18 mg, 0.05 mmol) in 3 mL of N,N-dimethylformamide (DMF) and 2 drops of NaOH (2 M) was sealed in a 15 mL vial and heated to 100 °C for 3 days, and then cooled to room temperature. The light yellow crystals obtained were washed with ethanol, and dried at room temperature (Yield: 60%). Anal. calcd for C63H73N9O19NaCd2.5: C 48.37; H 4.70; N 8.06. Found: C 48.01; H 4.48; N 7.72.
