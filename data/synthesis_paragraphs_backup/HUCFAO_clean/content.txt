Synthesis of complex 1: A mixture of Mn(OAc)2 (24.5 mg, 0.1 mmol), H3tci (10.35 mg, 0.03 mmol), H2O (1 mL), DMA (1 mL) and CH3OH (1 ml) were sealed in a 25 mL Teflon-lined stainless steel autoclave and heated at 80 °C for 3 days. And then colorless crystal of 1 was obtained in 36% yield. Elemental analysis (%): calcd. for C24H44Mn3N6O28 (1029.47): C 28.01, H 4.30, N 8.16; found: C 28.98, H 4.76, N 8.67.
