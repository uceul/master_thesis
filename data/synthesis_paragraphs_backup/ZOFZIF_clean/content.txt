
A mixture of MnCl2·4H2O (6 mg, 0.03 mmol), H4TADIP (5 mg, 0.013 mmol) and 70 μL of AcOH in DMF (4 mL) was added to a 20 mL vial, then the solution was heated at 110 °C for 48 h to give colourless block-shaped crystals.
