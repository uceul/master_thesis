
0.049 g (0.080 mmol) of H3L1 was dissolved in 4 mL N,N′-dimethylformamide (DMF) and the solution was acidified with 0.7 mL 1 M HNO3 in a 10 mL vial. 0.034 g (0.16 mmol) of Cu(NO3)2·6H2O was added to the above solution. The vial was heated at 70 °C for 2 d. After cooling to room temperature, the cyan block-shaped crystals formed were filtered and soaked in fresh DMF. Yield = 0.106 g, 91.0%. EA calc. for [Cu3(L1)2(H2O)3]·14DMF·16H2O (Cu3C102H178N20O51, FW = 2687.98), 1: C, 45.52; H, 6.67; N, 10.41. Found: C, 45.31; H, 6.16; N, 10.80%.
