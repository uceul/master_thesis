{[Co3(IDC)2(4,4‘-bipy)(H2O)4]·2H2O}n (1). A mixture of Co(NO3)2·6H2O (38 mg, 0.13 mmol), 4,5-dicarboxyimidazole (20 mg, 0.13 mmol), 4,4‘-bipy (20 mg, 0.13 mmol), and Et3N (0.036 mL, 0.26 mmol) in 15 mL of distilled water was sealed in a Teflon-lined stainless autoclave and heated at 160 °C for 4 days and then cooled to room temperature during 48 h. Red rhombus crystals (21 mg) were isolated in 66% yield and washed with distilled water. Anal. Calcd for C20H22N6O14Co3:  C, 32.13; N, 11.24; H, 2.95. Found:  C, 32.18; N, 11.20; H, 3.15.
