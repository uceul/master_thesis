A methanol solution (2 mL) of L2 (27 mg, 0.12 mmol) was carefully layered onto an aqueous solution (2 mL) of AgPF6 (15 mg, 0.06 mmol). Colorless crystals of [Ag(L2)2](PF6)⋅CH3OH formed at the interface in 4 days in a 59% yield based on the silver(I) salt. m.p. 171 °C (dec.). Anal.
