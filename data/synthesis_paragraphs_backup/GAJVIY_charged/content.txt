
A mixture of Zn(NO3)2·6H2O (0.300 g, 1.00 mmol), H2bpdc (0.480 g, 2.00 mmol) and [NH2(CH3)2]Cl (0.082 g, 1.00 mmol) was dissolved in DMF (30 ml) and heated in a Teflon bomb at 100 °C for 3 d. Colorless crystals were obtained, washed with DMF (2 × 10 ml) and diethyl ether (2 × 10 ml), and dried at 50 °C for 20 min. Yield 183 mg (36%, based on Zn). Anal. calc. for C75H83N7O21Zn3: C, 55.8; H, 5.2; N, 6.1. Found: C, 55.9; H, 5.0; N, 6.1%.
