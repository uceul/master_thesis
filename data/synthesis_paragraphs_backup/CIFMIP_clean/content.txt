A mixture of CoCl2·6H2O (0.238 g, about 1 mmol, pretreated under volume at 333 K for 1 h), H3BTC (0.066 g, 0.4 mmol), and phthalic acid (0.033 g, 0.2 mmol) in DMF (10 mL) was added into a Teflon vessel (23 mL). After ultrasonication for 5 min, solvothermal reaction was carried out in stainless steel autoclave at 393 K for 2 days. Blue needle-shaped single crystals of {Co3O(BTC)2(H2O)(DMF)}n (1) were obtained when the autoclave was cooled down to room temperature naturally (yield = 87% based on Co).
