
Zn(NO3)2·6H2O (0.1 mmol, 29.8 mg), 1,2,4-triazole (0.025 mmol, 2 mg), 2-aminoterephthalic acid (0.025 mmol, 4.5 mg) were dissolved in a mixed solvent of DMF (2 ml) and H2O (0.5 ml), then 0.025 ml HNO3 with a concentration of 62% was added, and the mixture was transferred to and sealed in a 20 ml Teflon-lined autoclave, which was heated at 90 °C for 48 h. After slow cooling to room temperature, colourless crystals of 1 were collected by filtration. Anal. calcd (found) for activated 1, C12H9O4N7Zn2: C, 32.31 (33.53); H, 2.03 (2.15); N, 21.98 (22.17)%.
