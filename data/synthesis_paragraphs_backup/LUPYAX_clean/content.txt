
A mixture of Zn(NO3)2·6H2O (0.18 g, 0.6 mmol), H2L (0.144 g, 0.6 mmol) was suspended in DMF (60 mL), and heated in a vial (100 mL) at 100 °C for 24 h. The colorless block shaped crystals formed was collected, washed with DMF, and dried in the air. Elemental analysis: Calcd. for Zn3(OH)(L)2.5(DMF)4 (C32H34N4O15Cl5Zn3): C, 35.33; H, 3.15; N, 5.15; Found: C, 35.06; H, 3.12; N, 4.75.
