 CuCl (102 mg, 1.05 mmol) and [H2L]Cl (120 mg, 0.349 mmol) were placed in a 20 ml vial and dissolved in 10.0 ml of NMP. The vial was placed in an oven at 120 °C for 20 h. Green rectangular crystals were collected and washed with NMP and cyclohexane. Yield: 105 mg (43.0%). Elemental analysis (CHN): calc. for [(C22H21ClCu2N3O5)·(C5H9NO)·(H2O)2]: C 45.99, H 4.86, N 7.95; found (%): C 45.93, H 4.22, N 7.91.
