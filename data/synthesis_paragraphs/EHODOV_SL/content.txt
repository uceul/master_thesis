‡ Synthesis of [In2(μ2-OH)2(obb)2]n·2nDMF(1): A mixture of In(NO3)3·4.5H2O (0.190 g, 0.5 mmol), 4,4′-oxybis(benzoic acid) (0.129 g, 0.5 mmol), tetrapropylammonium bromide (0.160 g, 0.5 mmol) and DMF (6 mL) was stirred for 10 min in air. Then the mixture was placed in a 20 mL vial and heated at 120 °C for 48 h. The vial was cooled to room temperature, washed with fresh DMF and colorless crystals were obtained. (0.180 g, 75% yield).
