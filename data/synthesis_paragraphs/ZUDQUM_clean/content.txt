A mixture of Mn(CH3COO)2·4H2O (0.1421 g, 0.6 mmol) and H3BTC (0.0672 g, 0.3 mmol), l-(+)-malic acid (0.0432 g, 0.3 mmol) and DBO (0.0393 g, 0.3 mmol) was in 3 mL DMF and 2 mL H2O was placed in a vial. The sample was stirred for 20 min and heated at 80 °C for 2 days. After cooling to the room temperature, colorless block crystals of 7 were collected by filtration and washed by DMF.
