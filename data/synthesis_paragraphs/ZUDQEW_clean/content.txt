A mixture of Cd(NO3)2·4H2O (0.1805 g, 0.6 mmol), H3BTC (0.0661 g, 0.3 mmol), d-Serine (0.0333 g, 0.3 mmol) and DBO (0.0347 g, 0.3 mmol) in 3 mL DMF and 2 mL H2O was placed in a vial. The sample was stirred for 20 min and heated at 80 °C for 3 days. After cooling to the room temperature, colorless block crystals of 4 were collected by filtration and washed by DMF.
