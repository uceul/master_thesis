A mixture of Mn(CH3COO)2·4H2O (0.1637 g, 0.6 mmol) and H3BTC (0.0597 g, 0.3 mmol), l-methionine (0.0599 g, 0.3 mmol) and DBO (0.0445 g, 0.3 mmol) in 3 mL DMF and 2 mL H2O was placed in a vial. The sample was stirred for 20 min and heated at 80 °C for 3 days. Colorless block crystals of 8 were isolated by filtration and washed by DMF.
