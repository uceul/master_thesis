A mixture of 0.37 g Co(CH3COO)2·4H2O (1.2 mmol), 0.25 g (1.5 mmol) pyridine-3,4-dicarboxylic acid, 0.27 g 1,2-bis(4-pyridyl)ethylene (1.2 mmol), 0.035 g (1.5 mmol) LiOH and 35 mL of EtOH/H2O (1:1) was sealed in a 45 mL vial and heated at 150 °C for 72 h.
