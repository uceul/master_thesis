 A mixture of H2MFDA (0.1 mmol, 28.2 mg), Eu(NO3)3·6H2O (0.1 mmol, 44.7 mg), DMF (5 mL), CH3CH2OH (2 mL) and H2O (1 mL) was sealed in a 15 mL Teflon-lined bomb and heated at 80 °C for 3 days. The reaction mixture was slowly cooled to room temperature. Colorless rod-like crystals of 1 suitable for X-ray diffraction analysis were isolated in 72% yield. Elemental analysis (%) calcd for C77H69N4O22Eu3: C, 49.77; H, 3.74; N, 3.02%. Found: C, 50.06; H, 3.57; N, 3.19%.
